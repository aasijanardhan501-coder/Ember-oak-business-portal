The Ember & Oak Story

Born from a Passion for Fire & Flavour

What started as Chef Marco Rossi's dream in a small courtyard kitchen has grown into the city's most beloved dining destination. Our philosophy is simple: respect the ingredient, master the fire, and let flavour speak.

Every piece of wood we burn, every herb we source, and every cocktail we shake is chosen with intention — to give you an experience that stays with you long after the last bite.

"Fire is the world's oldest cooking technique. We're just keeping the tradition alive — with a modern soul."

— Chef Marco Rossi, Founder
