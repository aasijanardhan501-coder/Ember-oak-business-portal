Passion Meets Purpose

About Us

From a small courtyard kitchen to the city's most celebrated dining destination — this is the Ember & Oak journey.

Est. 2018 · Mumbai
The Beginning

A Chef's Dream, Lit by Fire

Chef Marco Rossi grew up watching his grandmother cook over a wood fire in rural Goa. That primal connection between fire, food, and family became the foundation of everything he would create. After training in Michelin-starred kitchens across Europe — London, Barcelona, and Lyon — Marco returned to India with one mission: to bring world-class wood-fired cuisine to Mumbai.

In 2018, with little more than a dream, a secondhand wood-fired oven, and a small lease in Bandra, Ember & Oak was born. The first night had just 14 covers. Today, we seat 90 guests nightly and have a 3-week waiting list on weekends.

What has never changed is the ethos: respect the ingredient, master the fire, and let flavour do the talking.

The Journey

Milestones That Shaped Us

2018
Ember & Oak Opens Its Doors

We opened with a 14-seat courtyard restaurant and a single wood-fired oven. Chef Marco's philosophy was radical for the city — minimal intervention, maximum fire flavour. The first weekend was fully booked within 3 days.

2019
Craft Cocktail Bar Launched

Our bar programme launched with Head Mixologist Anika Singh bringing smoke-infused cocktails to life. The Smoke & Ember Old Fashioned debuted here and became an overnight sensation, featured in Condé Nast Traveller India.

2020
Pivoting Through the Pandemic

Like all restaurants, 2020 was our hardest year. We pivoted to premium home delivery with custom packaging, and launched Ember at Home — a DIY wood-fired kit. Our loyal community kept us alive.

2022
Forbes India 30 Under 30 — Chef Marco

Chef Marco was recognised in Forbes India's 30 Under 30 in Food & Beverage. We expanded to a 90-seat indoor-outdoor space with a private dining room for corporate events and celebrations.

2024
Best Restaurant — Time Out Mumbai Awards

Ember & Oak was named Best Restaurant 2024 by Time Out Mumbai, with special recognition for Best Bar Programme. We launched our seasonal tasting menu — a 7-course wood-fired journey.

2026
The Story Continues…

With a second location planned for Delhi, a cookbook in development, and a master-class series launching, Ember & Oak's best chapters are still being written.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

🔥
The Fire First

Every dish passes through our wood-fired oven. It's not a technique, it's a philosophy — fire transforms ingredients into something greater.

🌿
Locally Sourced

We partner with 14 local farms, fishermen, and dairies within 200km of Mumbai. Fresh, seasonal, and traceable — every single ingredient.

🤝
Community First

We employ locally, train in-house, and give 1% of revenue to Mumbai food banks. A restaurant that gives back is a restaurant worth building.

✨
Zero Waste Kitchen

Our kitchen composts 95% of food waste, uses seasonal menus to minimise waste, and all packaging is 100% biodegradable.

👨‍🍳
Craft Over Scale

We will never franchise or cut corners. Every sauce is made from scratch, every cocktail shaken by hand. Small is intentional.

💛
Hospitality as Art

We train every team member to remember guest names, preferences, and occasions. Hospitality isn't service — it's making people feel at home.
